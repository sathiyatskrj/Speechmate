## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Judge Q&A Defense

**Q: "You claim the app is 100% Offline, but what about the Community Hub? Doesn't sharing require internet?"**

**Answer (The Store & Forward Defense):**
"That is an excellent detail. 
The **Core Education Engine** (Translation, TTS, Learning Modules) is strictly **100% Offline** and requires zero signal to function in the classroom.
However, for the **Community Hub**, we use a **'Store & Forward'** architecture.
Teachers or elders can record stories and posts **offline** in the village. The app caches this data locally.
When the teacher travels to a connected zone (like the district office, typically once a week/month), the app automatically **syncs** these cached posts to the cloud.
So, **Usage is Offline**, but **Sharing is Asynchronous**. This ensures the app works in the deep forest but still connects to the world when possible."
